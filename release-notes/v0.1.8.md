# v0.1.8

Introduces full incident tracking — Pincer now records, groups, and displays incident history so you can review past outages and degraded periods.

## Highlights

- **Incident history** — Pincer now persists incident events across sessions, so you can review past outages even after restarting
- **Incidents page** — a new dedicated Incidents page shows incident cards and a timeline view
- **Retention cleanup** — old incident records are automatically removed based on your retention settings
- **Shared status colours and labels** — status indicators are now consistent across all views
