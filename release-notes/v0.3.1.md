# v0.3.1

The incident timeline now shows a colour-coded heatmap instead of individual check dots, making it much easier to spot patterns and problem periods at a glance.

## Highlights

- **Incident heatmap** — the timeline now displays a compact heatmap grid: 144 cells for the 24h view (one per 10 minutes) and 168 cells for the 7d view (one per hour)
- **Color-coded severity** — cells are shaded by error intensity, weighting failed checks more heavily than degraded ones
- **Time-period tooltips** — hover any cell to see a breakdown of ok, degraded, and failed check counts for that period
- **Horizontal scroll** — the heatmap scrolls horizontally when the grid overflows

## Fixes

- Fixed heatmap colors in light mode being too faint to read
